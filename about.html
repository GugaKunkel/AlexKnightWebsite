About Me

My name is Alex! I'm a filmmaker and adventure photographer based in Park City, Utah. I have a passion for the outdoors, adventure, and making the world a better place.

My passions include for photography, videography, hiking, and traveling.

I started photography at an early age and have never stopped since. I love to capture the beauty of nature and life itself.
